University of Wales

Previous Next

The University of Wales was a confederal university responsible for accrediting degrees for institutions in Wales and for courses provided overseas.

Working with James Box, and against a constrained budget and fixed timescale, we created an extensible design language that could be applied across the website, micro sites and the ‘myWales’ social e-learning network.
