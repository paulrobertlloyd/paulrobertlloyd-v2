Universal Networks

Previous Next

Design for a family of five sites representing NBC Universal’s international channel portfolio, including Syfy Universal, Universal Channel, 13th Street Universal and Studio Universal.

Working alongside James Box, I helped devise a common user experience and underlying design language that worked across different regional editions and also allowed each channel to exhibit its own unique brand personality.

Example implementations can be seen at:

  • Syfy Universal (UK)
  • Universal Channel (South Africa)
  • Studio Universal (Brazil)
  • 13TH STREET Universal (Spain)
