If you wish to contact me regarding new work, please use the form below – or just use it to say hello! Here are the services I provide:

  • Design: I believe that by developing common design and interaction patterns, we can combine the systems of the web and graphic design to great effect. I value cohesiveness and attention to detail, and designing products that can be used by all.

  • Front-end development: I employ pragmatic front-end architecture practices that progressively enhance semantic HTML with modular CSS and unobtrusive JavaScript. I advocate tools like style guides and pattern portfolios that encourage collaboration within teams.

  • Writing and speaking: I like to share accumulated knowledge with my peers. I have written articles for net Magazine, A List Apart and 24 ways, and spoken at design conferences around the UK and Europe.
